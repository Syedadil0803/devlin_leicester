FAQ's

Do you provide carpet installation services?

We do not carry out installations ourselves. However, over the past 35 years we have successfully referred experienced independent fitters whom many customers choose to hire for professional carpet and flooring installation across the local area.

If any concerns arise after installation, we will assist by contacting the independent fitter and helping arrange a review. While installation is carried out by independent contractors, we remain committed to supporting customers where possible.

Where is your showroom located?

Our showroom is located in Tamworth, where you can explore carpets, LVT, and other flooring options in person and receive expert advice from our team.

Are independent fitters part of your company?

No. Independent fitters operate as separate businesses. Any installation services are arranged directly between you and the fitter, although we’re happy to help facilitate communication if needed.

Do you offer free home measurements?

Yes. We provide free professional measurements to ensure accurate carpet or flooring estimates and help you choose the best options for your space.
